# Free Resources for B2B SaaS Growth

Everything you need to learn B2B SaaS growth. All free. No $200 consulting call required.

---

## PMF & User Research

- **[Y Combinator Startup School](https://www.startupschool.org/)** — Free self-paced course covering how to find an idea, build an MVP, talk to users, and get traction.
- **[How Superhuman Built an Engine to Find PMF](https://review.firstround.com/how-superhuman-built-an-engine-to-find-product-market-fit/)** — Rahul Vohra's step-by-step framework for measuring and systematically improving product-market fit. (First Round Review)
- **[The Mom Test — Summary](https://www.momtestbook.com/)** — Rob Fitzpatrick's official site for the book on how to talk to customers without leading them. Pair with free summaries on [ReadingGraphics](https://readingraphics.com/book-summary-the-mom-test/).
- **[Sean Ellis PMF Survey](https://pmfsurvey.com/)** — Free tool to run the "how would you feel if you could no longer use this product?" survey. 40%+ "very disappointed" = PMF signal.

## PLG & Product-Led Growth

- **[OpenView PLG Resources](https://openviewpartners.com/product-led-growth/)** — Hub of research, benchmarks, and frameworks from the firm that coined "product-led growth."
- **[ProductLed](https://productled.com/book/product-led-growth)** — Wes Bush's free PLG book and resources on building products that sell themselves.
- **[Growth Unhinged](https://kylepoyar.substack.com/)** — Kyle Poyar's weekly newsletter with data-backed PLG playbooks, pricing analysis, and SaaS benchmarks.
- **[Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/)** — Lenny Rachitsky's newsletter covering product, growth, and PLG strategy. Many posts are free; the [growth tag](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/t/growth?sort=top) is worth browsing.

## Sales & SLG

- **[SaaStr Blog](https://www.saastr.com/blog/)** — Jason Lemkin's massive library of tactical SaaS sales content, from first sales hire to scaling a revenue org.
- **[Gong Blog](https://www.gong.io/blog)** — Data-backed sales insights from millions of real sales calls. Covers discovery, negotiation, and deal execution.
- **[MEDDICC](https://meddicc.com/meddpicc-sales-methodology-and-process)** — Free breakdown of the MEDDPICC qualification framework used by enterprise sales teams.
- **[Pavilion (formerly Revenue Collective)](https://www.joinpavilion.com/)** — GTM leadership community. Blog and event recaps are free; membership is paid.

## Metrics & Analytics

- **[a16z: 16 Startup Metrics](https://a16z.com/16-startup-metrics/)** — Andreessen Horowitz's guide to the SaaS metrics investors actually care about (CAC, LTV, churn, burn, etc.).
- **[a16z Guide to Growth Metrics](https://a16z.com/growth/guide-growth-metrics/)** — Benchmarks for B2B growth, efficiency, retention, and margins.
- **[Christoph Janz's SaaS Funding Napkin](https://medium.com/point-nine-news/what-does-it-take-to-raise-capital-in-saas-in-2023-56d8f617714)** — One-page cheat sheet showing what metrics you need at each fundraising stage. Updated annually.
- **[David Skok's For Entrepreneurs](https://www.forentrepreneurs.com/)** — Deep dives on SaaS metrics, unit economics, and building a growth machine. Rated #1 entrepreneur site by Inc.
- **[Bessemer Cloud Index & Benchmarks](https://cloudindex.bvp.com/)** — Public cloud company performance data. Pair with the [Cloud 100 Benchmarks Report](https://www.bvp.com/atlas/the-cloud-100-benchmarks-report) for private company data.
- **[SaaStr Metrics Posts](https://www.saastr.com/author/jasonlkn/)** — Jason Lemkin's collected posts on SaaS metrics, milestones, and what "good" looks like at each ARR stage.

## Pricing

- **[ProfitWell / Paddle Pricing Content](https://www.paddle.com/resources/author/patrick-campbell)** — Patrick Campbell's data-driven pricing research, including the [Pricing Page Teardown](https://www.paddle.com/studios/shows/profitwell-report) video series.
- **[OpenView Pricing Insights](https://openviewpartners.com/blog/saas-pricing-insights/)** — Survey data from 2,200+ SaaS companies on pricing models, packaging, and usage-based pricing trends.
- **[Pricing Page Teardowns](https://www.paddle.com/studios/shows/profitwell-report)** — ProfitWell's video series breaking down real SaaS pricing pages with data and consumer research.

## Books (The Essential Shelf)

- **Obviously Awesome** by April Dunford — How to nail product positioning so customers immediately get why your product matters.
- **The Mom Test** by Rob Fitzpatrick — How to run customer conversations that give you real signal instead of polite lies.
- **Crossing the Chasm** by Geoffrey Moore — The classic on navigating the gap between early adopters and mainstream market.
- **Predictable Revenue** by Aaron Ross — The outbound sales playbook that scaled Salesforce's revenue engine.
- **Product-Led Growth** by Wes Bush — Framework for building products that acquire, activate, and retain users without a sales team.
