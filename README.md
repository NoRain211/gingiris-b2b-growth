## Case Studies & Sources

The original repo name-dropped these companies without linking sources. Here's where the claims actually come from — read the real thing:

| Company | Claim Used | Actual Source |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **HeyGen** | "1,800 user interviews to find PMF" | **Corrected.** The number 1,811 refers to Fiverr spokesperson listings found during market research, not interviews conducted. HeyGen validated demand by selling AI video services on Fiverr before writing code. Now at ~$100M ARR, $500M valuation, 100,000+ businesses. |
| **Deel** | "Partner program contributing $90M ARR" | Real but outdated. Sourced from a [Partnership Leaders webinar](https://medium.com/@partnershipleaders/the-secrets-behind-deels-partner-program-eb5281a60eff) with Tai Rattigan. Deel has since surpassed **$1B total ARR** (Q1 2025), $300M Series E at $17.3B valuation. Partner-attributed ARR is now ~$200M+. Note: Deel is involved in a [major corporate espionage lawsuit with Rippling](https://www.reuters.com/legal/) (March 2025), escalated to DOJ investigation (January 2026). |
| **Vercel** | "'The Frontend Cloud' category creation" | Historically accurate, but Vercel officially pivoted to **"The AI Cloud"** at Vercel Ship 2025. [$300M Series F at $9.3B](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20250930898216/en/Vercel-Closes-Series-F-at-%249.3B-Valuation-to-Scale-the-AI-Cloud). v0 has 4M+ users, ~$42M estimated ARR. |
| **Supabase** | "Build in a weekend, scale to millions" | Tagline is actually **"Build in a weekend. Scale to billions."** Now at ~$70M ARR (250% YoY growth), $5B valuation, 4M+ developers. Growth fueled by the "vibe coding" wave (Bolt.new, Lovable, Cursor). Real growth story in [Craft Ventures' inside account](https://www.craftventures.com/articles/inside-supabases-product-community-led-growth). |
| **InVideo** | "50% first-order high commission" | That's just [their affiliate page](https://invideo.io/make/affiliate-program/): 50% on monthly plans, 25% on annual. It's a commission rate, not a growth insight. |
