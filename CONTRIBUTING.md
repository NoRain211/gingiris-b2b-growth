# Contributing

This playbook is better when people with real experience contribute. Here's how.

## What We're Looking For

**High priority:**
- Native speaker improvements to Japanese and Korean translations
- Real-world war stories and examples (anonymized is fine)
- Industry-specific playbooks (fintech, healthtech, devtools, etc.)
- Corrections to benchmarks or outdated information
- Additional CSV templates people actually use

**Also welcome:**
- New language translations
- Improvements to existing frameworks
- Links to free resources we missed
- Anti-patterns from your own experience

## What We're NOT Looking For

- Promotional content or links to paid products
- Consulting CTAs or lead-gen content (that's what we forked away from)
- Vague "thought leadership" without actionable substance
- AI-generated fluff to pad line counts

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repo
2. Make your changes
3. Open a PR with a clear description of what you added and why
4. If adding benchmarks or data, include your source

## Translation Guidelines

- Keep English technical terms where they're standard in the target language (PMF, PLG, SLG, CAC, LTV, etc.)
- Keep company and tool names in English
- Translate naturally — don't be overly literal
- Match the tone: direct, practical, no fluff

## File Structure

```
references/
├── en/           # English (primary, most complete)
├── ja/           # Japanese
├── ko/           # Korean
├── foundation.md # Chinese (root = Chinese)
├── growth-engine.md
├── value-presentation.md
├── contracts.md
├── metrics.md
└── seo-geo-guide.md
templates/        # CSV templates
```

## Style

- Be direct. Lead with the answer.
- Include real numbers and benchmarks where possible.
- If you reference a framework, show how to actually use it.
- No filler, no preamble, no "in today's fast-paced business environment."
